About

We strive to elevate the burger experience to its top shelf - because you and your burger deserve better. While other “burger joints” serve their burgers in wax paper, or in a cardboard box, GeeksBurgers holds itself to a higher standard. Burgers this great deserve to be served stacked high on a real plate, at a table with a proper setting, and with a great glass of wine or a craft beer, by a person who speaks the language of real service. A GeeksBurgers Burger could never be eaten standing up. Knife and fork is optional napkins are required.
